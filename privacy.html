Privacy Policy

La Saga Manager — Last updated: February 19, 2026

1. Data Collection

La Saga Manager does not collect any personal data from third-party users. The app is used exclusively by the band's management team.

2. TikTok Data

When you authorize this app with TikTok, we access only the following:

  • user.info.basic: Your TikTok display name and avatar (used to confirm the correct account).
  • video.publish: Permission to post videos to your TikTok profile.
  • video.upload: Permission to upload video drafts to your TikTok account.

We do not access your followers, messages, likes, or any other personal information.

3. Data Storage

Access tokens are stored securely on a private server and are used only for publishing content. We do not share any data with third parties.

4. Data Deletion

You can revoke this app's access at any time from your TikTok account settings. Upon revocation, all stored tokens are automatically invalidated.

5. Contact

For privacy-related questions: lasagaweb@gmail.com
